PROGRAMS

Class Descriptions

Strength & Power

Build maximum strength through compound movements like squats, deadlifts, and bench press. Progressive overload programming designed to increase your one-rep max and overall power output.

60 minutes Intermediate to Advanced High Intensity

HIIT Bootcamp

High-intensity interval training combining cardio and strength exercises. Torch calories, improve cardiovascular fitness, and build lean muscle in this challenging full-body workout.

45 minutes All Levels Maximum Intensity

Olympic Lifting

Master the snatch, clean & jerk, and related movements. Develop explosive power, coordination, and technique under the guidance of certified Olympic lifting coaches.

75 minutes Intermediate to Advanced Technical

Boxing Technique

Learn proper boxing fundamentals including stance, footwork, punching technique, and combinations. Great cardio workout while developing coordination and mental focus.

60 minutes Beginner to Intermediate High Intensity

Functional Fitness

Multi-joint movements that train your body for real-life activities and sports. Improve mobility, stability, and overall athletic performance with varied, constantly challenging workouts.

60 minutes All Levels Moderate to High

Mobility & Recovery

Active recovery sessions focusing on flexibility, mobility, and injury prevention. Includes foam rolling, dynamic stretching, and yoga-inspired movements to optimize recovery.

45 minutes All Levels Low Intensity
