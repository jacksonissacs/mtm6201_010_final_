About APEX

More than a gym. A community of champions.

OUR STORY

Built on Performance

Founded in 2015, APEX Performance Gym was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to elite-level training and coaching.

What started as a small warehouse space with a handful of dedicated athletes has grown into one of the region's premier performance training facilities. Our founders, former collegiate athletes themselves, recognized a gap in the fitness industry - too many gyms focused on aesthetics over athletics, machine work over movement quality.

Today, APEX stands as a testament to what's possible when you combine expert coaching, premium equipment, and a community that pushes each other to be better every single day. We've helped hundreds of athletes reach their goals, from everyday fitness enthusiasts to competitive powerlifters and professional athletes.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Our Core Values

Excellence

We hold ourselves and our members to the highest standards. Every workout, every rep, every coaching cue is an opportunity to pursue excellence.
